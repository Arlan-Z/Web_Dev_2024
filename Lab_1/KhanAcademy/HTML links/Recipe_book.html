Recipe #1

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: Serve hot
Ingredients Quantity
Tomatoes 2
Sauce 90 g
Olive oil 5g
Pizza dough 1
Spices by your choice
Toppings of your choice (ham, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, etc.). by your choice

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 220 degrees Celsius (428 degrees Fahrenheit). Roll out the dough on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

Step 2: Evenly spread tomato sauce over the surface of the dough, leaving edges for the crust.

Step 3: Distribute sliced tomatoes and your chosen toppings evenly across the pizza. Sprinkle a thin layer of shredded cheese on top.

Step 4: Season with spices to taste (salt, pepper, oregano). Drizzle olive oil over the pizza for aroma and moisture.

Step 5: Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven. Bake the pizza at 220 degrees Celsius for approximately 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
